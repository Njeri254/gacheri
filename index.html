SAVE THE WORLD

Around Kenya, especially the rural areas, too many children still start a life at a disadvantage because of who they are and where they come from.
Our Concern is how the children around Kenya are caught in the middle of conflicts. Climate Change, COVID-19 and conflicts in homes have left many children vulnerable to critical levels of hunger, poverty and insufficient education in Schools.
Some of those in shelters and homes, in prisons and hospital beds have families that have lost hope and completely abandond them and we all can do something about it.
Help US protect Kenya by ensuring they not only survive but smile, thrive and become the best versions of themselves.

What We Do

School Tours

In an effort to ensure breakthrough, we offer health education, guidance and counselling to children in Schools with the help of well wishers and volunteers. We provide shoes, clothes, sanitary towels and overall health commodities to the least fortunate.

Poverty

We visit children in the Orphanages, elderly homes, prisons, hospitals and people living with disabilities, with an effort to bring hope. Through donations and members contributions, the money raised is used to buy basic needs like food and clothing. We also gather resources and do a soup kitchen once in a while.

Livelihoods

Through donations and savings, we hope families will be financially stable and able to support and strengthen their communities.

Rescue Shelters

We provide spaces where the least fortunate can have food, shelter and clothing with a hope of building long lasting rescue shelters.

Child Sponsorship

For only Ksh.50 and Ksh.100 a day you bring a lasting change in a child's life by providing food and access to education programs for a child.
