About Us

In a world subtly divided in negativity, where douts rule and fear conquers the anxious minds, we all need each other to live in positive energy.
Every donation goes to children in schools, to the vulnerable through finances, natural foods from farms and clothes.

Our History

I am a SERMON is a Charity non-profit organization founded in 2018 driven by the need to see a lasting change in the society, change in how the world treats the least fortunate and the need to create national relief societies made up of volunteers, trained in peacetime to provide neutral and impartial help to relieve suffering of the needy children and the elderly. The name “I am a SERMON” was inspired by the need to attract and show the mission of the Organization as one that ministers to the people. Volunteers were needed to help the vulnerable with chores and good hygiene. A structure of schools and orphanages in the county was adopted and extended the existence of the Organization. The Organization was able to stand on itself at the end of 2018 by consistent donations. Through donations, the Organization is able to give employment opportunities in different departments to ensure a workable environment to reach a wide group of people and attract people from around the Country to achieve its mission.

Our Mission

To inspire breakthroughs in the way the world treats the needy children and very least fortunate in the society.


Our Vision

A world in which every child and least fortunate families attain the privilege to survival, protection, development, participation and in the end, a good life.
