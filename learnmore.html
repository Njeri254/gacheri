Maji Mazuri Children's Home, Kasarani - Nairobi

This is one of the many homes around Kenya. Due to a very large population in the county, children in this home are fortunate enough to get shelter, clothes and education at their center from well wishers and volunteers. The orphanage is able to accomodate the vulnerable children with limited access to basic needs through donations from well wishers.
