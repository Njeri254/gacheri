Volunteer

You can make a difference

There are different many ways you can help out the Community and be a key part of I am a SERMON's Mission and Vision. Together, we can do alot of things.

Gathering tangible resources and Essentials.

Individuals can come together and gather tangible resources such as clothes, shoes, food and toiletries that will be delivered to Children and homes around Busia. Volunteers make our distribution journey successful by loading resources into moveable items. For the ones priviledged with vehicles can also help by offering means from one location to another.

"By sharing my resources with the least fortunate, I get to appreciate every small thing I am priviledged to have."

- Collins, Music Director at the Anglican Diocese of Nairobi, Nairobi

Help at places we visit

I am a SERMON serves the elderly and the vulnerable. These services include cleaning up of their spaces. This program is a great one for people who are passionate about helping the vulnerable to get the services that may sometimes be a hindrence to their stay.

"Volunteering with I am a SERMON makes me feel useful and keeps me involved."

- Ethan, Professional Footballer, Nairobi

For more info about volunteering and signing up, please visit our Volunteer Page.
