School Tours


In our events, we make it a priority to visit atleast two schools in a month, within Busia County.

Boys

School-going boys go to school struggling with poverty, inadequate education about their health and in turn, loosing concentration during studies. In an effort to help, we gather finances and ensure atleast one child has uniform, shoes and toiletries. We also offer health education and guidance counselling to those that struggle in their homes. Indoing this, children are able to study will less distractions.

Girls

School-going girls go to school struggling with poverty, inadequate education about their health and in turn, loosing concentration during studies. In an effort to help, we gather finances and ensure atleast one child has uniform, shoes and toiletries (sanitary pads). We also offer health education and guidance counselling to those that struggle in their homes. Indoing this, children are able to study will less distractions. In an effort to ensure that cultures against education for females will be reduced and all girls are able to learn.
