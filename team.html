Leadership Team

The Leadership Team is responsible for ensuring smooth operations at I am a SERMON and strengthen the organization's operations.

Philip Huru

Director, Founder and Chief Executive Office

Philip Huru is the Founder and Director of 'I am a SERMON' since 2018 driven by a need to see a lasting change in the Society. He is an Anglican and a Chaplain at St. Thomas ACK Cathedral, Nambale. He is also a teacher at Nambale Boys Secondary School, Nambale-Busia.

Through his involvement in Schools and Churches, he identified the needs of some children in Schools and the vulnerable, he envisioned a world where every child attains a right to survive. He opened the Organization and runs it.

Maureen Njeri

Software Developer, Operation Manager

Maureen Njeri is the Operation Manager. She joined the Organization later October 2018 as an IT intern and was interested in being part. She started out as a volunteer and later on, raised on the ranks.
